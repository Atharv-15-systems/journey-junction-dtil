Journey Junction - App Workflow

Journey Junction is your ultimate companion for safe and enjoyable trekking and hiking adventures. Explore the app features below!

1. Welcome Page

A friendly introduction to our platform. Users are greeted with information about Journey Junction.

2. Login Page

Securely log in to access the app features or create a new account to join the Journey Junction family.

3. Dashboard

Access all features of the app, including navigation, shopping, emergency services, and live tracking.

4. Requirement Page

Purchase hiking and trekking essentials like tents, ropes, compasses, and more from our integrated store.

5. Location Tracker

Navigate with live maps, track coordinates of other users using their tracking ID, and get real-time directions.

6. Get My Location

Check your current location, view coordinates, and track your speed to understand your position better.

7. Check Guide Availability

Ensure your safety by finding available guides for your destination before you start trekking.

8. Emergency

Quickly contact mountain rescue, share live coordinates, or find other emergency services during a crisis.
